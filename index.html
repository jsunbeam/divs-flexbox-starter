I was born and raised in NJ but currently reside in MN. I miss living near the coast and seeing the ocean, eating a wide variety of ethnic foods, and being near my family. However, I do not miss the pace of life. The Twin Cities offer the perfect mix of a slower pace of life with a vibrant arts scene and community life.

My Hobbies: I love playing computer games with friends online to de-stress. I have also recently started getting into jazz piano. As a classically trained pianist, jazz is a completely differently world to me, and I am loving it!

Latest obsession: Checking out all the farmer's markets in the summertime and revelling in the fresh flavors of locally grown produce.
